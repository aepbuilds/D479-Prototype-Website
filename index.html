Welcome to Taniti

Taniti is a small, tropical island paradise nestled in the Pacific Ocean. Though less than 500 square miles in size, Taniti offers an impressive variety of landscapes—from sun-drenched sandy beaches and rocky coastlines to lush rainforests, a safe natural harbor, and a mountainous interior featuring a small, active volcano.
Home to an indigenous population of around 20,000, Taniti has traditionally thrived on fishing and agriculture. In recent years, however, it has emerged as an exciting and welcoming destination for travelers seeking both adventure and tranquility.

What Awaits You on Taniti?

Diverse Cuisine

Savor fresh local seafood, comforting American classics, or flavorful Pan-Asian dishes at one of the island’s ten restaurants.

Comfortable Lodging

From budget hostels and cozy B&Bs to a four-star resort, there's a place for every traveler.

Unforgettable Activities

Lounge on white sand beaches, explore the rainforest, hike to a volcano, go snorkeling, zip lining, or enjoy a vibrant nightlife with pubs, art galleries, a dance club, and more.
